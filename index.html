Personalised expertise, worldwide reach

Our experienced team and carefully selected service providers deliver tailor-made solutions across Africa and the globe, managing every aspect from customs clearances to complete cold chain logistics with diligence and precision. We know the complexities of this industry backwards — and we’re here to make it simple, efficient, and effective for you.

Efficiency is our currency

In an industry known for its fluidity and complexity, Port Freight offers the calm assurance of solutions that work. We understand that in logistics, time is the true cost. Our processes are built to minimise delays, maximise efficiency, and ensure your cargo keeps moving, no matter the challenge.

A heritage of trust

Family-owned and operated, Port Freight has been part of the foundation of the customs and logistics industry for over half a century. Our connections, experience, and hands-on approach mean that when challenges arise — and they will — you have a partner who knows how to get things done.
