A 50 year legacy

For over 50 years, Port Freight has been at the forefront of clearing, forwarding, and logistics in South Africa - not just moving cargo, but moving with purpose, precision, and partnership.

We’ve navigated every kind of cargo challenge, regulatory shift and market cycle this industry can throw - and through it all, we’ve remained a trusted constant for our clients. Where others promise, we perform. From day one to delivery, we’re on it.

What sets us apart is more than our legacy. It’s how we work.

We bring an unmatched depth of knowledge, built from decades in the field. We understand the nuance of each commodity, the pressure of timelines, the complexity of border regulations, and the critical value of compliance.

But we also understand people. We offer hands-on service, tailored support, and a level of responsiveness that’s become rare in this space. Our clients know they can reach us when it matters - and that we’ll get it done, no matter what.

In a fast-moving and often confusing trade environment, we bring clarity, control and confidence. From citrus to textiles and beyond, we’ve built our reputation on care, compliance and consistency - and we don’t cut corners.
