perishable / cold chain logistics

Sensitive cargo demands sensitive care - and specialist expertise. We offer fully integrated cold chain logistics, including temperature-controlled storage, monitoring, and express clearances. Our in-depth understanding of SARS procedures, HS classifications, and international trade protocols enables us to navigate even the most complex customs scenarios. From import permits to tariff reclassification and rebate applications, we handle the red tape so your goods move without disruption.

Whether it’s full container loads, groupage, breakbulk or time-critical air freight, we manage your cargo across every mode and region. Our door-to-door solutions include document management, insurance, compliance, and proactive tracking — ensuring visibility, reliability, and peace of mind.
